Strife's world is teaming with life. There are the good guys, bad guys and plenty of those that are simply out to tear your torso apart at the joints. Pretty picture, eh? Just remember, not everybody in Strife has it in for you. Interact with Non Player Character's (NPC's) to gather information and clues to your role in this hostile land. Oh...speaking of, wait until you get a load of the Rat King. You're gonna love him. And the Stalkers, Crusaders, the Governor and...well...you get the point.

Oh, let us bring up one more: "Blackbird! Mmmmmm." Curious?
