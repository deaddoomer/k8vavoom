The Sigil
The five pieces of the Sigil are won in combat from various foes within the world of Strife. The order in which you gather them is crucial to the game. These two lists discern between the Dark Path and the Light Path. Choose wisely.

Sigil Pieces

Light Path Dark Path
First Programmer Programmer
Second Bishop Bishop
Third Oracle Macil
Fourth Macil Loremaster
Fifth Loremaster Oracle

Pay attention to whom you kill, and you'll save the planet. Otherwise, you'll become an empty hulk of a gamer with an entire world's blood on your hands.
