Strife Minutiae
- SIGIL PIECES -
 The weapon of power has five pieces to attain from five enemies. Depending on how you get them, you could end up travelling down more than one road. This list will lay it out for you.
- STAT INCREASES -
 One of the more delightful aspects of Strife is the possibility of honing the player character's abilities. This list will tell you when you'll be able to do so.
- KEYS -
 By the end of the game, you'll look like a janitor with all of these hanging from your belt.

Perhaps not the most exciting stuff, but if you're into knowing it all...you're into knowing it all.
