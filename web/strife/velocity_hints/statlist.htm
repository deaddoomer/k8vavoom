Stat Increases
There are ten stat increases in the game. You can get all of them only if you take the "B" path - kill the Oracle then see Macil. The following is a list of when, where and how you receive them.

  1. Complete Prison Mission (Macil awards tokens)

  2. Complete Power Station Mission (Irale and Medic in town award instants)

  3. Complete Sewers (Macil awards tokens)

  4. (OPT): Complete Training Facility level (switch at end awards, instant)

  5. Complete castle mission- kill Programmer (Macil awards tokens)

  6. Kill the Bishop (Oracle awards tokens)

  7. a. Kill Macil (Oracle awards tokens) or
    b. Kill the Oracle (Macil awards tokens)

  8. (OPT, b path) Shut down transmitter in mines (instant)

  9. (b path) Shut down peasant converter machine (instant)

  10. Kill Loremaster (instant)

NOTE: If you kill the Oracle before Macil, you will continue down the B path. If you kill Macil first, B path is not available. OPT are optional stat increases. All stat increases award 1 Stamina and 1 Accuracy. Each increase raises your Accuracy and Stamina values by 10 (out of a possible 100 total for either).
