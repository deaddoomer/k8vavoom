Keys

There are plenty of locked doors in Strife, if you want to open them you'll need these. These are all of the keys in the game and where to find them.

  1. Base key: Rowan (Near Sewage plant in town-2).

  2. Govs key: Macil (Front Base).

  3. Passcard: Governor Mourel (Governor's home in town-2).

  4. ID Badge: Prison door guard (Prison entrance-5).

  5. Prison key: Warden (Upper level, prison-5).

  6. Severed hand key: Judge Wolenick (Lower level, prison-5).

  7. Power 1 key: Movement spy (Warehouse of the power station-4).

  8. Power 2 key: Technician (Upper level of power station-4).

  9. Gold key: Technician (Power station-4).

  10. Power 3 key: Technician (Core area of power station-4).

  11. ID Card: Sewer guard (Beginning area of the sewers-6).

  12. Silver key: False Programmer #3 (Audience Chamber in Castle-8).

  13. Oracle key: Guards right outside of the temple (Borderlands-11).

  14. Military ID: Keymaster (Borderlands-11).

  15. Order key: Guard inside base and warehouse filed guard (Bailey-17).

  16. Warehouse key: Sergeant Guard (Bailey-17).

  17. Brass Key: Macil (New Front Base-10)

  18. Red Crystal Key (Conversion Chapel-24)

  19. Blue Crystal Key (Conversion Chapel-24)

  20. Chapel Key (Conversion Chapel-24)

  21. Catacomb Key : Richter (Order Commons-23)

  22. Security Key: Quincy (hospital area of the Fortress:Bailey-17)

  23. Core Key : Acolyte (near water area in Fortress:Administration-15)

  24. Mauler Key: Warehouse guard (Stores-17).

  25. Factory Key (ruins-25)

  26. Mine Key (ruins-25)

Keys, passcards and severed body parts...yep, quite a game.
