Cheats For The Teaser

(These will NOT work on the retail version):

IDMYPOS - shows position

IDMUS## - changes music (track number)

IBGOD - Invulnerability

GUNS - All weapons

OPEN - keys added

LISTIT - power ups

STUFF - ammo satchel

STIC - no slide to stop

SPIRIT - No Clipping mode

GOTO## - Level Warp

KILLEM - Kill all inhabitants on level

TIC - turns on devparm mode

MOVEME# - moves your position to player start(0), or player entrance (1 - 6)

MONEY - gives you 300 gold
